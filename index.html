Our Story

A Legacy of Music Since 1991

Music Cabin is one of the oldest musical instrument stores in the twin cities of Hyderabad & Secunderabad. Founded in 1991 by two friends bound by a shared passion — serving musicians with devotion and integrity.

For over three decades, we have been the trusted destination for artists across Telangana. From the beginner strumming their first chord to the seasoned professional seeking perfection, Music Cabin has always been there — with quality products at affordable prices.
